Cognition evolves with your application

Cognition

Cognition is the beating heart at the core of Stretchr. It is a patent pending, unique, artificial intelligence engine that has only one purpose: ensuring access to your data is as quick as possible. Applications naturally evolve, so we designed to Cognition evolve with them. It monitors performance and proactively reorganizes, distributes and indexes your data to minimize response times.

With Cognition on your team, you never need to worry about indexing, sharding or anything else database related. Think of it as your personal Virtual Database Administrator.
